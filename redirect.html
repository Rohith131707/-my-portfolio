GitHub Pages

You can also deploy your portfolio using GitHub Pages:

  1. Go to your repository: https://github.com/Rohith131707/-my-portfolio
  2. Click on "Settings" tab
  3. Navigate to "Pages" in the left sidebar
  4. Under "Source", select "main" branch
  5. Click "Save"
  6. Wait a few minutes for deployment to complete

Your site will be available at: https://rohith131707.github.io/-my-portfolio/
